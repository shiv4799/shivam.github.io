ABOUT

Vision is the most awaited techfest for every student in SVIT. This Year Computer Department presents you VISION 7.0., A legacy of our department to provide the best symposium experience since 6 years. Vision gives you a platform and opprtunities to take part in different events and activities.

Tech Non-Tech Workshop

TECH EVENTS

ROUND-1: Snap-Check:

Description: In this round, we will show pictures related to technology (present, future & some basic questions). The participants will do the analysis of pictures and options will be given if necessary in the given printed page.

ROUND-2: Python 1:

Description: In this round, we will give a brief introduction to the participants related to Python language and in remaining time questions will be given in page related to what had been taught and the one with the most appropriate answers will be qualified for the last round.

ROUND-3: Python 2:

Description: In this round, participants who are qualified have to implement the coding of Python which was taught in the previous round and PC will be provided by us. And the one with appropriate answer will be declared as winner.

NOTE: ROUND-2 and ROUND-3 is a combinational round.

ROUND-1: CALL-GATE:

DESCRIPTION: In this round, participants are provided with a question paper which has various logic gates diagram. The participants have to find out the output of all questions. At last, the participants have to aggregate the final output of all questions and has to apply BODMAS rule on it and has to find out the final answer.

ROUND-2: CODE-EN-CRYPT:

DESCRIPTION: In this round, we will give a common username to the participants and they will have to decrypt the password using a grid which will be available in a printed page. After that the link will be opened by the participants and in it another username will be given. Similarly, they have to decrypt another password and lastly a riddle or a puzzle will be given as a final quest. The one whose answer is correct will proceed to the next round.

ROUND-3: MIME-CODE: (DUO ROUND):

Description: In this round, one of the team members will be on the PC wearing headsets and another will solve the questions and has to explain the answer to the partner. He/she has to understand it and has to put that to fill the blank which is available on screen. The group with the most correct output will be declared as the winner.

NON-TECH EVENTS

ROUND 1: FLICK-A-LIX

Participants will be shown a video clip that will be from different movies/TV series for total of 6-7 minutes, at the end they will be handed a question paper with questions related to video shown.

ROUND 2: SNAP-VENGERS

Participants will be divided into team of 5/6 and will be given a word related to Marvel Universe and a Map of our college. Teams will have to go to those particular places from the map and make the letters (in a Human letter form) from given word.

ROUND 3: THE MAD TITAN’S QUEST

Participants will be in a team of 5/6. Teams will be given clues from which they will have to find the Infinity stones. After collecting all the Infinity stones they will have to come back to put those stones in the Infinity Gauntlet. Team to do this first will reign supreme

ROUND 4: RAPID FIRE

Immediately after winning the last round, that one team will now compete among themselves in a rapid fire and only one will be crowned ‘The champion’.

EVENT 1: DIG IT UP!

There will be bucket full of sand in which items of different categories will be there, participant will have to take out one item at a time and put it in a box according to its category.

EVENT 2: TRIP DOWN A MEMORY LANE

The classroom will be filled with images of different things, participants will have 2 minutes to go in and remember as many things as possible. After coming out we will give them a Letter and they will have to tell us things starting with that letter from the room.

EVENT 3: THE WALK

Participant will have to walk the path while wearing a VR glass that will show them some adventurous vision.

ROADIES

Winners from the looping event will be competing in this surprise round.
